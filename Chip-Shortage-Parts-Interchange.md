## Proteus MCU options

Requirements:
- LQFP144 
- 1MB flash (but 2MB works as well)
- STM32F4: Known working is any F427/F429.  256KB RAM required. 1MB or 2MB acceptable.
- STM32F7: Known working is F767. 1MB or 2MB acceptable.
